What is Thermage FLX?

Thermage FLX is the latest generation of Thermage, a revolutionary non-surgical skin tightening treatment that uses radiofrequency (RF) energy to heat the deeper layers of skin. At Cheongdam DEAR Skin Clinic in Gangnam Seoul, we provide professional Thermage FLX treatments with genuine FDA-approved equipment.

The FLX (Fourth-generation) version features AccuREP Technology, which optimizes energy delivery for more comfortable treatment and better results. Unlike other treatments that require multiple sessions, Thermage FLX delivers visible results in a single treatment.

How Thermage FLX Works

Thermage FLX uses monopolar radiofrequency technology to heat the deep, collagen-rich layers of your skin while the tip simultaneously cools and protects the outer layer. This heating action causes immediate collagen contraction (immediate tightening effect) and stimulates new collagen production over the following months (progressive improvement).

Thermage FLX vs Ultherapy

Both are excellent non-surgical lifting treatments. Key differences:

  • Technology: Thermage uses RF energy / Ultherapy uses ultrasound
  • Results: Thermage shows immediate effect / Ultherapy gradual over months
  • Treatment depth: Thermage heats broader area / Ultherapy targets specific depths
  • Best for: Thermage for overall tightening / Ultherapy for precise lifting

Many patients combine both for optimal results. Our doctor will recommend the best approach.

Frequently Asked Questions
