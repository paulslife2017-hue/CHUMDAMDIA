Frequently Asked Questions

Is filler treatment painful?

We apply numbing cream before treatment. Most patients feel only mild pressure. Some fillers contain lidocaine for added comfort.

How long do fillers last?

Results typically last 6-18 months depending on product type, injection area, and individual metabolism. Lips and moving areas break down faster than static areas like cheeks.

Can fillers look natural?

Absolutely! Our philosophy is natural enhancement. We focus on balanced proportions rather than overfilled appearance. You'll look like the best version of yourself.

What if I don't like the results?

Hyaluronic acid fillers can be dissolved with hyaluronidase enzyme if needed. However, with proper consultation and expert technique, this is rarely necessary.

Do you treat international patients?

Yes! We regularly treat patients from around the world. English-speaking staff will guide you through the entire process from consultation to aftercare.
