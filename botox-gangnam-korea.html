Frequently Asked Questions

Is Botox safe?

Yes. Botox has been used safely for over 30 years with millions of treatments worldwide. When administered by trained professionals using authentic products, it's very safe.

Will I look frozen or unnatural?

Not with proper technique! We aim for natural-looking results where you can still make expressions but with softened wrinkles.

How long does Botox last?

Typically 3-6 months depending on area treated, dosage, and individual metabolism. Masseter Botox may last 4-6 months.

When will I see results?

Initial effects appear in 3-7 days, with full results visible at 2 weeks. Masseter reduction takes 2-4 weeks to become noticeable.

Can I combine Botox with fillers?

Absolutely! Botox and fillers work synergistically. Botox relaxes muscles (dynamic wrinkles) while fillers add volume (static wrinkles).

Do you treat foreigners?

Yes! We regularly treat international patients. English-speaking staff will guide you through everything.
