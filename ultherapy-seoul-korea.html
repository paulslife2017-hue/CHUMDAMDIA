Frequently Asked Questions

Is Ultherapy painful?

Most patients experience some discomfort during treatment, described as a prickling or warming sensation. Our clinic can provide pain management options to ensure your comfort.

How long does Ultherapy last?

Results typically last 12-18 months. Some patients see benefits for up to 2 years. Many patients return for maintenance treatments annually.

Can I combine Ultherapy with other treatments?

Yes! Ultherapy works well with treatments like Thermage, dermal fillers, Botox, and laser treatments. Our dermatologists will recommend the best combination for your goals.

Am I a good candidate for Ultherapy?

Ideal candidates are adults with mild to moderate skin laxity. During consultation, our doctor will assess if Ultherapy is right for you or recommend alternative treatments.

Do you accept international patients?

Absolutely! We regularly treat patients from around the world. Our English-speaking staff will guide you through the entire process, from booking to post-treatment care.
